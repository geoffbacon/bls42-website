Friday 5th, Saturday 6th & Sunday 7th February 2016

Dwinelle Hall, UC Berkeley.

We'll do our best to find places for all students, although in the event we cannot, we'll give preference to those coming internationally.

Transport

San Francisco International Airport (SFO) to Berkeley
  • Bay Area Rapid Transit (BART)
    Take a Pittsburg-Bay Point-bound (yellow line) train from SFO and transfer to the Richmond-bound (red line) train across the platform at either 19th St/Oakland or MacArthur stations. BART runs on weekdays from 4AM to 12AM, on Saturday from 6AM to 12AM, and on Sunday from 8AM to 12AM. The Berkeley campus is located one block east of the Downtown Berkeley BART station. A one-way trip from SFO to Downtown Berkeley is $9.25.
  • Shuttle service
    For shuttles we recommend making a reservation at least 48 hours in advance. Rides can be reserved online or over the phone.
    Bayporter Express Shuttle: Direct from SFO to Berkeley from $38 (shared ride).
    Super Shuttle: Direct from SFO to Berkeley from $35 (shared van).
  • Uber
    Fare approximately $65
  • Taxi
    Fare approximately $90

Driving

From I-80/580 take the second Berkeley exit at University and head eastward (away from the bay and marina). Continue east on University for about 2 miles, then turn right onto Shattuck. Go 7 blocks and turn left onto Channing.

There are several commercial parking garages in the area about three to five blocks uphill:

  • on Bowditch on the south side of Bancroft
  • on Bancroft between Telegraph and Dana (next to the Student Union)
  • on Channing or Durant between Dana and Telegraph.

There are also University parking lots that can be used on evenings and weekends. See the following links for helpful information:

  • UC Berkeley Parking and Transport office
  • City of Berkeley parking information
